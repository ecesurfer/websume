Portfolio

TrueFitTattooSD.com

TrueFit is a very prominent tattoo shop in College Area of San Diego. I wrote the site in a Ruby on Rails, CoffeeScript, HAML stack. The client, Tomas Archuleta needed a site that would be extensible and allow him to add artists. Also he wanted artists to be able to manage their own images as they created their art.

Qualcomm ChipCode | A Fork of GitLab

Currently I'm on a small team of developers at Qualcomm working on ChipCode, a GitHub fork of GitLab. I've added several features to this project on the front end including a brand new front page, Solr search integration, help and blog section as well as many back end features.

MyMFTHours.herokuapp.com

The client noticed that there was no free way to track client hours for her MFT (Marriage and Family Therapy) degree. It allows users to track hours in a calender view from FullCalendar.js. Once users have hours tracked they can order and total their hours using pre-programmed BBS rules.

Passweb.herokuapp.com

I decided I wanted to access my passwords from anywhere, securely. I designed and developed Passweb using Ruby on Rails. Passwords are secure using 2-way AES-128 encryption with the hash key compiled between user data from the database, and strings from two separate servers.

5Fish iOS App

As a university senior capstone, I lead a team to design and build a prototype of the 5Fish iOS app currently on the App Store for the Global Recording Network (GRN). The app is designed to download, play and share a Gospel message in over 6000 languages.
